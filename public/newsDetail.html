Jetinno: Pioneers Of Coffee Innovation At HOST 2023

Oct 10, 2023
View: 780

Temukan Masa Depan Penyeduhan Kopi

Milan, Italia - Jetinno, otoritas terkemuka dalam teknologi mesin kopi, siap untuk memikat para pengunjung di HOST 2023, pameran perhotelan utama di Milan. Dengan sejarah sejak tahun 2013, Jetinno secara konsisten mendorong batas-batas inovasi peralatan cerdas, terutama di bidang mesin kopi komersial.

Jetinno: A Decade of Excellence

Jetinno, established in 2013, has consistently been at the forefront of crafting state-of-the-art solutions for the coffee industry. From vending to HORECA and OCS deployments, Jetinno has earned a trusted reputation in the field. With two production centers, four research labs, and one R&D center, Jetinno possesses the infrastructure necessary to cater to a global market.

Boasting over 60 professional engineers and an annual production output of 100,000 machines, Jetinno is well-prepared to expand its global presence. The company's dedication to innovation is evident through its collection of over 80 technology patents, positioning Jetinno as a forward-thinking industry leader.

JL15 dan JL30: Masa Depan Penyeduhan Kopi

At HOST 2023, Jetinno will unveil the JL15 and JL30 series of coffee machines, designed to elevate your coffee experience: Jetinno: A Decade of Excellence

Timeless Elegance Meets Innovation: These machines seamlessly blend classic aesthetics with advanced brewing technology, making them the perfect addition to any coffee-serving establishment.

Perfectly Brewed Coffee: Whether you're a seasoned barista or a coffee enthusiast, the JL15 and JL30 ensure that each cup is a masterpiece, with precision and consistency that set new standards.

Rich and Aromatic Flavors: Jetinno's commitment to quality guarantees that every cup is brimming with the rich, aromatic flavors that define exceptional coffee. Reliability and Efficiency: Crafted with Jetinno's expertise, the JL15 and JL30 series offer reliability and efficiency, making them the ideal choice for commercial use.

Temukan Masa Depan Penyeduhan Kopi

Jetinno cordially invites coffee enthusiasts and professionals alike to visit Stand V59, Hall 22, to experience the future of coffee brewing. Don't miss the opportunity to savor a perfect cup of coffee and witness Jetinno's unwavering commitment to excellence.

We extend a warm welcome to you to join us at HOST 2023 in Milan, where Jetinno will redefine your coffee experience.
